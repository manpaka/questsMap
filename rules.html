Guidelines and Rules

What are the West Marches?

  • A West Marches campaign is more of a sandbox. Any major plot sessions are opt in, most sessions are started by player decisions.
  • There is no regular schedule: every session is scheduled by the players.
  • Each session has different party members from the larger group. There is normally a limit on how many can play based on the session and DM.

Recaps

Recap Guidelines

When a session finishes one or more players can write up a session recap for the community to read. When a recap is posted the player character is given a reward of gold or items based on the session and level.

  • Only one recap per session, per player
  • Any recap that is written in character, the player gets a better reward.
