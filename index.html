Warforged in Amber

May 1st

With the sudden loss of Decax the party had to figure out their next step. The next step that was decided was to continue on with their plan to travel up the mountain towards what they knew as the location where both Sergei and Decax's souls went. With Kipna's skills in traversing mountains and forests the journey was short. The group took the direct route through the mountainous terrain, at the highest point a huge creature was spotted in the distance, a Roc. Thankfully the titanic bird had not spotted the party, as it was quite distant. The party descended down the mountain with Kipna and Lil-kip keeping them hidden from the Roc, and finding paths to get the party down the mountain safely. The journey lasted only a few hours, it ended with the party arriving at the foot of a massive temple. The temple was carved into the mountain and measured over fifty feet in height. On the far left side of the temple was a large fissure that had formed, this fissure was what the party used to enter the building.

Inside the temple the party found that large amounts of the building was built from Amber. Inside a nearby hall there were three green flaming skulls, which the party decided to not fight. The party continued deeper to the center of the temple. In the central chamber, at the back are a set of double doors made of amber, on the sides are several halls, and in the center back of the chamber stood a large statue of a robed figure in a spellcasting pose. Areyja discovered that, in the entrance for the two side halls, are pairs of amber mirrors where their reflections would act irregularly. The reflections would wave and gesture for the person to not enter deeper. Not going into the main side halls, the party decided to investigate the two sets of double doors made of amber at the back of the central chamber. One set of doors had collapsed and led into an empty room. The other set of doors were locked with an arcane lock spell. With a clever usage of the spell silence and a strong barbarian, the amber doors were broken open. Inside the room were six large piles of treasure. As some of the party entered the room, a huge invisible force struck Stormfang. A massive invisible golem made of amber had been placed in the room to guard the treasures. The tough fight eventually ended with the invisible amber golem destroyed. The room was searched and many treasures were found. On one of the piles sat a gem encrusted skull, which Cambyses picked up. Upon picking up the skull, Cam was compelled to move to an unknown chamber in the temple. Moving through a fissure at the back of the chamber, Cam went up a large set of stairs and into an old library, whose walls were covered in ancient tomes. Cam went to one of the walls that was found to have a secret door, which led to a dusty but nice study. In the center of the study sat a headless skeleton wearing a torn yellow cloak. Cambyses was compelled to place the skull onto the body, against the party's better judgment. Placing the skull on the skeleton, Cambyses' trance had left his mind, with him realizing that the skeleton might be a potentially powerful lich. The lich rose from the chair and thanked the party for their help in awakening them. The group then asked several questions and learned several things.

The amber temple was built by a group of wizards who wanted to seal away many evil entities and forbidden knowledge. Long time ago Strahd von Zarovich had come to the temple and made a pact with one of said entities, said pact leading to him becoming a vampire. In the temple sits many amber sarcophagi, each containing a powerful entity that offers powers to those who accept them. The lich has little to no strong thoughts on Strahd. The party also asked the lich who they are, to which they responded that they don't know. But the lich does know information on the amber temple, which they said that they can guide the party through the temple without getting attacked by the temple's protectors. The party asked the lich about the fate of the souls of Decax and Sergei, to which the lich responded that they would be in “collections.” The lich brought the party back to the central chamber in the temple. After showing the party some of the amber sarcophagi in one of the side halls, the lich brought them to a magically locked door that led to the tomb of the wizards who had built the temple. Soon the party was brought to one of the tomb's amber alcoves. In the seemingly empty alcoves were the souls that had been collected by the temple over a period longer than known. The soul of Decax was pulled out of the amber by the telekinetic hand of Cambyses, and returned to his body.

The Paladin, though quite confused, was brought back from his imprisonment in the temple. With that resurrection the Lich brought the group to one of the many vaults of amber sarcophagi. The three that were inspected each offered a dark gift. The Finger of Oblivion, The Power of Evasiveness, and the Gift of the HellHound Whisperer. Though the gifts were tempting, the group decided to not take them. The task of the Lich was done, at least to them, and with their memory returning. The group was then asked to leave by the lich.

The group started to leave through the exit and back into the snow, Decax decided to turn back and ask the lich something. The paladin's loud clanking had brought the attention of stormfang who had turned around to check on Decax. The two stepped onto the balcony to see the lich on the main floor, the figure that stood there had a green pustule covered skin growing onto the skeletal figure. A crown shaped form, made of skin and bone, grew onto the skull. The lich then began to float as their arms became outstretched to the sides. The large statue had changed as well, its pose imitating the lich. The lich, just before vanishing said “I remember…”

They have been reawoken
This will become a hazardous situation when they return...

The Cursed Village

April 17th

A scream was heard outside of Madam Eva's tent. The adventurers rushed out and saw what was causing the commotion, they saw living shadows draining life from multiple Vistani. From the fire pit in the center of the camp a flame skull rose. The fight ended quickly with the newly given powers that the adventurer's were given. Stormfang, wielding the legendary Sunsword, used their feline agility to rush the undead. The fight quickly ended, with the undead being taken out quite quickly. Once the fight was over, the party had decided to get Kipna a magical bow from Madam Eva so that the Ranger could more effectively damage resistant creatures.

After a short rest at the camp, the party moved in the direction of the Village of Barovia. On the way there a ghostly yellow figure was spotted in the distance. Stormfang, due him partially recognising the figure. Moving forward to properly identify the elven figure, the Tabaxi became worried about who the potential ghost was. Once he got closer, the ghost was found to not be identifiable outside of the familiar traits that Stormfang could recognise. Before anything else could be discovered the glowing yellow ghost vanished upon a loud distant scream echoed out and was suddenly cut out mid scream without even an echo to say that it was there. Spooked, the party continued on towards the Village of Barovia.

Getting to the dismal village that sits in the shadow of the massive castle of Ravenloft. The group then headed to the local tavern to rest for the coming journey. Inside the tavern, the bartender Malik, was asked about a passed out individual in the corner. Malik remarked that the character was some drunk named Ismark, another outsider to Barovia who had come with a couple others. Ismark, who was shocked awake by a bucket of water, told the party that he was from Baldur's Gate and was supposed to join a small mob of villagers to go up to Castle Ravenloft to kill the vampire Strahd. But had not left with the mob due to him passing out from drinking.

A few of the party, Stormfang, Sander, Decax, and Cambyses, split off to go up to the local church and deliver the clothing they had gotten way back at the old bone grinder. They arrived at the old withered building they met with Father Donavich. He had told the group that it was a blessing that they killed the witches, but it did not change what the village people had done. As they were complicit in their kids being taken from them. After taking the clothes and promising to bury them, Father Donavich then asked Decax if they had any holy magic to aid him. The reason for the sudden question was that he needed someone with holy magic to cleanse his son Doru of an affliction he recently got. The affliction was vampirism. Father Donavich led the party to the chained trap door leading to the church's basement so they could potentially cure Doru. Cambyses, after discovering that curing vampirism required more magic than Decax had at the time, suggested use of a small magical item that he had gained when he entered Barovia with Sander and Decax. The bead itself could produce an effect similar to the spell greater restoration, but had another unknown effect. The four entered the basement and found a withered and starved vampire spawn. Using the Sunblade to prevent Doru from getting closer the group was decently confident that they would not be attacked as Decax prayed to his god. But what they did not fully expect was that Doru was starving. The vampire spawn jumped at decax but was not expecting that Decax being a Warforged did not, in fact, have any blood. As Doru grappled Decax Cambyses decided to use the red bead he was carrying. The bead disintegrated in Cam's hand and Doru suddenly burst into flame which did not burn the vampire. While the others were focused on Doru, Cam suddenly was hit by unseen damage which spiked his body. Stormfang held the Sunsword up closer to Doru to see if he would burn from the sunlight that the sword radiated. Doru did not burn from the sword which is what the group hoped for, the kid was freed from his vampirism and connection to Strahd. Doru was brought back upstairs. The priest is overjoyed to see the miracle that had been bestowed to his son.

As the group sat around Father Donavich and Doru, a slow clap started behind them from the front door of the church. It was Strahd von Zarovich, he had arrived to congratulate the party for their deed. As the others sat in front of the Donaviches, Strahd frustratingly admitted that he had not noticed that the party even had the bead. Knowing what the bead does to the user, Strahd said that he is tempted to test the effect the bead had caused, almost charging the group with his claws. But instead, Strahd retracted his claws and took a different approach. Strahd looked toward Stormfang and the Sunsword. He pulled out of his coat pocket a deck of familiar cards, at least to Stormfang it is familiar. The deck was the Deck of Many Things that had caused a lot of trouble for Stormfang and the ones he was with before Barovia. Decax, being curious about such an item, decided to pull a card from the deck. The card he pulled had a locked door covered in chains with Void written on it. The other's had barely a second to react as Decax collapsed onto the floor. Before the party could then attack Strahd in revenge, the vampire gave them pause as he admitted that he would reveal where the Warforged's spirit had gone. The vampire pulled a different deck out and pulled two cards out of said deck. The first card was the Tempter and the second was the Transmuter. The vampire sarcastically wished them luck before vanishing.

Another piece has been taken off the board.
He is out of reach, in a trap that will end this false adventure...

For a Moment, The Sun Returned

March 20th

Areyja and Ireena were there in front of Strahd. The Vampire had an expression of death in his eyes but before he could strike the bard down he had a single offer to make. Strahd reached into his cloak and made his offer, Areyja would let Strahd have Ireena and he would not kill her or the rest of the party, he also pulled out a familiar lute in front of the bard. This lute was an item that belonged to an old mentor of Areyja's. Enraged, Areyja decided to fight the vampire due to his offer, and then the fight began.

The rest of the party had started to bolt towards Areyja as she backed away from Strahd and his spawn. They were not even slowed down by a massive minotaur skeleton that Strahd had created. Decax stayed behind at the tower to fight the undead monster, quickly destroying it. The party was fast, Stormfang sprinting with his feline agility and then jumping onto a wildshaped Sander. Though they were not fast enough, as Areyja and Ireena were alone in the fight. The bard though got aid from a new direction, Hadrian and the Professor appeared in the sky on Hadrian's flying staff. The two had begun to cast spells to slow the vampires, Hadrian using a large tidal wall of water and the professor putting a thick acidic gas around Strahd. These spells seemingly did not hurt Strahd himself, as his spawn grappled Areyja and Ireena, he then walked forward and put the bard nearly into the grave with a single swipe. Areyja, sensing her approaching death, casted a spell of flying onto her ally. At that moment, Strahd made his offer one last time. The Bard only needed to drop the spell and he would let her live. Areyja braced herself as she started to build her magic into a single teleportation spell to save Ireena; but she was interrupted though. A ball of sunlight had appeared in the sky above the fight, Hadrian and Professor Wynather had seen the opportunity to eliminate the vampire spawn and their master. The two took the chance and Hadrian used all of his dormant celestial power to create a beacon of light powerful enough to illuminate Barovia itself. Professor Wynather then enhanced the beacon of light with her own magic. As the two moved closer to Strahd, Hadrian said one last thing to the professor,

“Sorry I couldn't teach you celestial,” to which she replied calmly,“Don't worry, I think we will have all the time in the world”

The beacon of light descended down towards the vampire lord, as he screamed in pain from the light, a pair of giant wings appeared and engulfed him. Then a bright flash of light shot outward. When their vision had returned, the party saw only Areyja and Ireena left. On the ground there lay only a few items, the two artifacts that they had found in Barovia, and a pair of glasses. A bead of light was seen then entering the parted clouds and into the breaching sunlight. The party had lost two of their own.

After some time the remaining adventurer's left the peninsula and decided that it is time to seek out Madam Eva. They headed eastwards towards Vallaki but stopped short of the actual town. Ireena was from there and said that it was destroyed by Strahd and his vampire spawn. But the town was still there, seemingly untouched by the slaughter that had occurred a few nights ago. Cautiously, and after some scouting by Kipna and Lil-kip, the party elected to sneak around the edge of the town and not enter it. As they continued walking they soon came across a figure on the road. The figure was seemingly a dwarf of some kind, they were still in their slumped posture. Decax elected to go forward first. Upon approaching the figure he illuminated their face to reveal that it was the corpse of a familiar person, who had been lifted off the ground and impaled on a massive wooden pike. Decax dropped to his knees as the party asked him who it was. He told them that it was Thelkahm Glofdenn, the dwarf who had built him, his father. As he got closer to the impaled body it animated and began attempting to murder the closest figure to it. Decax did what he had to, as he pulled out his sword and slowly drove it into the corpse's body, smiting it and utterly destroying the magic that had animated it. They then buried the body and their hatred of Barovia deepend.

The party traveled far, passed Castle Ravenloft, and over the bridge at Tser Falls until they spotted a side road. Traveling down, in hopes of a quicker journey to the Village of Barovia, the party spotted a small campsite. The campsite had several carts and tents pitched up next to the river and a large bonfire in the center. There was music being played by the folk who were staying there, the music was played with an accordion. As they got closer a younger man, dressed in a brightly colored cloak carrying a long staff with a lit lantern on the end, called to the travelers to join in the festivities. It became obvious that these folk were Vistani, and that they held no ill will towards the adventurers. Several of the adventurers decided to join in the music, Areyja with her tin whistle, Sander with a ram's horn bugle, Stormfang with a Lyre, and Decax dancing along. Kipna laid down and took a quick nap on Lil-kip. The night was young and the time spent was generally uplifting for most. Cambyses asked the one who greeted them, who was named Owen, if Madam Eva was staying at the campsite. He replied she stayed in the largest tent there but was not ready for visitors just yet. Owen, after being asked about them, told the adventurers a couple interesting details about the Vistani. The most interesting detail was that they were allowed by Strahd to leave Barovia, but only the Vistani could come and go as they wished. The arrangement was allowed purely because they provided Strahd with information of the outside worlds. When asked about where they stay in the Swords Coast Owen reveals the most surprising piece of information, the Vistani have never visited Faerun and do not know what the Swords Coast is. But before further questioning Owen said that it was time for the adventurers to meet Madam Eva. The tent was entirely covered in furs, trinkets and many tapestries. The timeless woman behind the central table turned and told the party that she was expecting them, even though they were much later than she wanted. Stormfang mentioned that Madam Eva holds a sword of sunlight, she mentioned that if she did hold something that powerful she would not be too upset if the party found it in her tent. Though, Madam Eva had one condition, they are to trade something with her for the sword. She was then shown the letter that Stormfang and Areyja were given some time ago by Azen Stormleaf. She accepted the letter through gritted teeth, mentioning that Azen had passed through a while ago. Azen had a small potted plant with him but he was not let back into the campsite. So, the party began to search the tent for the sword. The party found many items of high value, but Eva would only let them take the sword. Soon though a lone platinum long sword hilt was found it was missing its blade. Stormfang being the one who most craves the power that it holds, having heard stories of such a weapon. The blade ignited in a brilliant shining length of pure radiant light. As everyone stared at the blade in awe a sound was heard outside the tent. The campsite was being attacked, but the party now had a weapon to fight the vampire.

Though they expect undead they will be getting something far more… malicious?

Loss of the Resurrected

March 6th

The four adventurers Sander, Decax, Cambyses, and Stormfang followed Ireena Kolyana to the pool in the small village in Kresk. She said that she was being called towards the magical pool by an unknown force. As the pool came into sight it was noticed that a storm had begun to brew. Ireena, after being asked about it, said she did not know what drew her to the place but that it had a familiar and warm sense to it. The group is observed as a spectral hand raised from the water, the hand beckoning Ireena towards it. She started to approach it, ignoring the worried questions of the other four. It became quickly obvious that she was not being enchanted due to some unknown forgotten memories. The hand raised more out of the water to reveal a ghostly figure, their body bearing long black hair and noble features. The ghost had a striking resemblance to the Vampire Lord of Barovia, but there was something else to this specter. Ireena, seeing the figure's true form, then started to sprint towards the specter, with full intentions to enter the pool and potentially drown. Stormfang, in a moment of worry, suspected a trap and stopped Ireena with his battleaxe, Hew. The storm began to hit the area in a thunderous roar with lightning striking the village. Ireena was not able to join the familiar specter in time and it sank below the waters as Ireena screamed towards it. An angry voice boomed from the sky screamed “No” as the figure disappeared under the water. The four did not have time to react to the voice as a massive bolt of lightning shot down from the clouds above and struck the pool. The pool's magic did not survive the lightning, and a small mote of light was spotted shooting up from the pool and into the skies, it then flew to the southeast towards Mount Ghakis. Ireena became silent while Stormfang tried to apologize to her, only for her to drop to her knees. Stormfang knelt next to her and could only ask,
That was Sergei, wasn't it?”

Near the entrance of Kresk, Areyja and Kipna sat at the bar, drinking while Areyja played some music. The other adventurers had soon appeared as the rainstorm outside had begun to get more heavy and violent. Ireena was slung over Stormfang's shoulder, she had become despondent after what had happened. The fighter put her down onto a chair and again asked her some questions about what had happened. Ireena would not respond; then Stormfang went into a full inspiring monologue which ended with him telling her to get up and find Sergei. She finally responded and said that she has no memory of who Sergei is or why she is drawn to him. The night ended with Ireena joining the party to take down the Vampire Strahd. After that, in the nearby inn, the party began to take a rest and planned to leave towards the southeast to find Sergei. During said rest, Cambyses mentions a theory he's had, "Potentially, when a soul dies in Barovia it does not leave into the outer planes but becomes trapped only be reincarnated in Barovia"
The party then rests fully; but before heading southeast like they planned, it was decided they take a detour and investigate a tower near lake Baratok.

At the foot of the large four story tower, it is found that the front door had no door knob or handle but a weird disc covered in several images. Cambyses noted that the images look like hand and arm motions. Upon doing said motions the door opened and they explored the tower, with Lil-kip flying up and landing on the roof. The investigation led to the top of the tower where the party found several things, a suit of magically animated armor, a warm metal stove, and a chest with a decapitated head in it. The head was identified as Azen Stormleaf, a vistani wandering merchant that had previously given Stormfang and Areyja a card reading that had aided them in the party's journey through Barovia. His head was perfectly preserved if a bit off putting. On the other side of the room it was discovered that saying a keyword, “Khazan”, near the suit of armor would bring it to life and follow the commands of the creature who had said the keyword. Stormfang was the one to say the keyword. As the party continued to explore the tower, several of them descended back to the third floor of the tower. This floor had a large whole in the side facing the lake and Ireena was sitting in the opening. Before something could be said, the tower creaked and a loud snap was heard; the tower was collapsing. In the moment Cambyses casted the spell Feather Fall to save the party while Areyja grabbed Ireena and used the spell Dimension Door to teleport the two to the midpoint of the road, five hundred feet away from the tower. Standing right next to the two that had teleported was Strahd von Zarovich, who was flanked by three vampire spawn. He had collapsed the tower, and had a look of rage in his eyes.

The lovers had seen each other again but sadly now… Strahd has come to claim his prize.

The Dead near and in Kresk

February 6th and 20th

The party had gained a new artifact. The Holy Symbol of Ravenkind. Hadrian was given the holy symbol and Icon of Ravenloft due to his abilities as a cleric meshing quite well with the two artifacts. This had fulfilled what was found on the Enchanter Card given to the party by Azen Stormleaf of the Vistani. After leaving the swamp the group spotted a large ruined mansion on a hilltop. Ascending, it was found that the ancient ruin was home to a group of very depressed undead revenants, with their violent leader Vladimir Horngaard,. The party was informed by a nameless revenant that if the group needs to meet the leader they can meet with Sir Gwilym. Sir Gwilym was a tall half-giant warrior-priest of the Silver Dragon Argynvost. He informed the party that he and Vladimir are the last ones to even remember their names. Gwilym told the party that the mansion used to be owned by the late Argynvost who's magical power could create a powerful beacon of light for all of Barovia to see. Gwilym, after telling the party about the mental state of the revenants, Gwilym is offered to pat Lil-kip on the head. The revenant's eternal undeath was brightened for a brief moment because of Lil-kip and his soft feathers. The priest then told the party that if they returned the skull of Argynvost to the manor and reunited it with the rest of the skeleton can create another beacon which could weaken the Vampire's influence on Barovia's citizens. Before the party had left the haunted mansion they handed the Tome that they found in the swamp to Gwilym for protection. The revenants had never invited Strahd to Argynvostholt which allowed the manor to be a safe haven for the party and the tome. Gwilym was confedent that strahd will not be able to get the tome while it is in Argynvostholt.

Leaving the manor and deep into the forest the party encountered a lone wandering man. The Professor went up to the wanderer to ask them who they were. The man said that the lord of Barovia knows that they have taken his tome, and he demands for it to be returned. After telling the party the bounty from Strahd, the man suddenly transformed into his hybrid werewolf form. It was an ambush by a band of werewolves.

The lycanthropes had been told by Strahd to kill the party but after they put up a formidable resistance the fight ended. The chief lycan had been told by Stormfang that if he kept fighting then he and his band would die. The lycan had only one condition of his surrender, that he and the rest of his lycanthropes could take their dead away, the party agreed, against Hadrian's wish to kill the lycanthropes. Though the werewolves got to take their dead, the spellbook of their magic user was taken by professor wynather. After cleaning themselves up, the party finally arrived at the town of Kresk. At the front gate a woman armored in an intricate half-plate set was yelling at the guards to let her in. The party arrived behind her and were able to convince the guards that the woman was with them, after questioning her. The party then learned about the woman; her name is Ireena Kolyana, she was related to the Burgomaster of Vallaki and had escaped the town when it was being attacked. She was being called to Kresk after spending a considerable amount of time in the woods and fighting undead alone. The group splits into three groups; kipna and areyja head to the local inn to rest and eat while professor wynather and hadrian go up to the abbey. stormfang, cambyses, decax, and sander then continue on with Ireena towards a mysterious glowing pool at the north side of Kresk.

The professor and hadrian arrived at the front door of the Abbey of Saint Markovia, they were greeted by the malformed humanoid creature named Otto Belview, who had the legs and half the face of a canine-like creature. With Otto was the shy character known as Zygfrek Belview. The two were escorted to the front door where they met the Abbot of Saint Markovia. A young and elegant figure that welcomed them to the abbey, he then began giving them a tour of the abbey. Hadrian uses a detection spell and learns that the abbot is some form of celestial. The first, and only, room they arrived into was the main hall. The hall had a large table with a single veiled humanoid female sitting at it. She was swaying to the low ambient music box that was playing on the upper balcony overlooking the room. The abbot said that her name is Vasilka, and that she requires something. The abbot looks at the Professor and mentions that her face is what is required, as Vasilka does not have a face. Upon questioning, the abbot confesses that Strahd has trapped him in Barovia until he can make the vampire lord a bride and Vasilka is not his first or probably last attempt. The Professor declined the request for Vasilka to have her face, which the abbot tried to offer that he will give his ability to revive someone from the dead without cost. The two then leave after declining the offer. Hadrian then uses a spell of divination to ask the Dawnfather if the abbot's quest to make Strahd a bride will actually work; then a voice responded after a delay, “Only, if he succeeds will he die.”

All he need was a face...
So that he may finally die...

Magic Under the Moss

January 16th and 23rd

The evening sets in as the party rests for the night. Upon the sun rising and poking through the clouds the party buried the bodies. The group had mourned the unknown dead and stayed in the field for some time. Investigating the area had shown that several stones and rocks around the field are tombstones. The field is an ancient battlefield turned graveyard.

They then decided to head towards the nearby ruins of Berez to continue following their lead to some sort of holy symbol. The hill descended downwards to a low marsh area with several ruins peaking through the heavily moss covered plane. Hadrian, Professor Wynather, and Areyja stay at the top of the field as the rest of the group heads down the hill. As the party starts to move onto the moss Stormfang, who immediately sinks into a hidden sinkhole. He becomes encased under several feet of silt and water and about to drown only to be pulled out from the sinkhole by the party. This showed the party that the field is covered in a large amount of hidden sinkholes that could result in a creature getting trapped under the ground. There were two buildings visible, half sunken into the moss field, a large building in the center of the field and a large tower in the distance. The group split into three teams; Stormfang and Kipna went to a small ruin nearby, Decax and Sander moved toward the large building in the center of the field, and Cambyses stayed behind to ritually cast the spell detect magic. Sander and Decax, while moving towards the manor, ended up finding themselves in a sudden sinkhole. Decax quickly saved Sander using magical grasping roots. Sander, being able to talk with animals, asks a small group of Skinks to help the two continue onwards without falling into any other sinkholes.

Stormfang and Kipna investigate a small ruin poking out of the moss. They discovered that the ruins, which are barely poking out of the moss, could be potentially over three floors tall. Cam stood nearby as his detect magic spell finished. During which Areyja came down the hill to meet up with Cambyses, Stormfang and Kipna. Areyja was pointed towards the tower in the far distance. She used her Fochlucan Bandore to cast the spell fly and she flew towards the tower. As Areyja was flying towards the tower Decax and Sander arrived at the large manor in the center of the ruins. The two then began to enter the building and saw, at the floor that they had access to, there was a noticeable water stream flowing downwards to a potential basement. Cambyses, after finishing his ritual cast of detect magic, caught up with Sander and Decax at the large building. The three entered the building. As they followed the flowing water stream, Cambyses smelt a familiar scent of smoke and entered a room that brought memories of his old life in Baldur's Gate. Cambyses ran out of the building into the open air, being followed by Sander and Decax. The three were soon joined by Stormfang, Kipna and Lil-kip. Stormfang had gotten a message from Areyja.

Areyja had arrived at the tower, it looked like a watch tower of some kind that had sunk several floors into the ground. Areyja, being able to fly, landed on the top floor balcony. She saw that there was a large nest with a single egg in the center. Upon inspection she concluded that it is either a Gryffon nest or hippogriff nest. She floated above the nest to get to the door across the room, along with grabbing a potion that was visibly sitting in the nest among other trinkets and bones.
