Group Merger

December 5th Recap

In the wild and dark land of Barovia an adventure begins with the meeting of two groups...

After a long journey the five adventurers, areyja, hadrian, stormfang, the professor, kipna, and the giant owl lilkip had turned a corner to see the village of Kresk in the distance. It was midday and Hadrian's vision saw the unknown figures approaching the village in the evening. While moving towards the village Stormfang is grabbed and carried away by one of the Wareravens they had previously met outside of Vallaki, Adrian Martikov. Soon the entire party was grabbed, Kipna jumped onto Lil Kip mid-flight, and Hadrian had already started using his staff to follow along. The group was brought to a large Vineyard, known as The Wizard of the Wines. stormfang had arrived early to aid in the battle while the rest of the group had arrived at the very end, and there were some interesting new faces at the vineyard.

Before they had arrived there was another group already there, cambyses, decax, and sander rainpath. The three wanted entry into Kresk a little bit ago and were told to go to the vineyard to see why there were no more shipments, then maybe they'd be let into the village. They engaged a cult of insane druids who had infested the winery with Blights using a Gulthias Staff. Soon the three were joined by an early arrival, stormfang. After ridding the building of the blights, they noticed a larger force coming in from the south. As they prepared for a fight, cambyses discovered that the staff, upon being broken in half, would instantly kill all blights within 500ft of it with an ear piercing scream. To which the Tiefling snapped the staff on one of the building roofs.

The two groups soon converged and began having a discussion on their next course of action, along with methods of taming the large amount of ravens in the winery. hadrian flies off to tell the martikovs that their winery is cleaned out, along with that he gets some info about the Crawling Hut of the Witch, which he saw in the distance... Hadrian gets back the group and they continue to talk about ravens, but are cut off when they notice that areyja is missing. They start to look for the bard and a couple of them notice that she is out to the west, running into the forest.

Areyja had seen someone she knew and began chasing after the supposed apparition. The party chased after her, except Cambyses who was helping the Martikovs with cleaning. Soon they stop her deeper into the forest and see an oddity, Areyja sees the instrument of her teacher, Decax sees the body of juice, Sander sees the ashes of the wolf snarl, Kipna sees the body of Lil Kip, and Hadrian sees a small meat pie. Stormfang and the Professor see the illusion for what it is, a deep hole. Which closes immediately after the Professor pokes it with a stick. Ending the illusion.

The party soon went back to the winery. Areyja stays rather quiet, as Stormfang had aggressively held her back from grabbing the illusory instrument. They then had a couple options,

  • Go to the ruins of Berez and look for a supposed item that can aid them in Barovia,
  • Go back to Kresk for safety with a shipment of wine, or
  • Fight the wandering tree.
They decide to fight the tree.

They start their journey, Areyja is silent, and Hadrian is keeping track of the crawling tree. The party splits ways with the martikov wine cart and heads south east. After some time, they notice that the tree is climbing the hill up ahead. The party climbs the hill and soon see the tree stop at the center of the flat grass covered hilltop. The group then approaches the tree and sees that clearly lit front door. Stormfang, Decax, and Sander then go into the tree.

Inside the tree, Stormfang notices a shelf with a large book on it. He grabs the book and the tree suddenly becomes active as the door slams shut. Outside the party sees the door slam shut and the tree uprooting itself. As panic sets in, a massive ring of red light appears around the tree. Looking outside of the ring, a couple dozen ghostly blue humanoid forms appear.

The night is coming, and it will be a long night...
